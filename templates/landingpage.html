Accidental Analysis

Description

A powerful tool designed to assess the likelihood of accidents occurring in a specific area based on user-provided inputs. Whether you’re a city planner, a safety consultant, or a concerned citizen, this web page offers valuable insights to enhance safety measures and decision-making.

Key Features

Accident Analysis (2016-2023)

Explore detailed graphs and statistics on road accidents in Karnataka over the years. Our analysis covers:

  • Weather Conditions: Understand how different weather conditions impact accident rates.
  • Road Characteristics: Analyze accidents based on road types and conditions.
  • Accident Locations: Examine the distribution of accidents in various locations such as highways, urban roads, and rural areas.
  • District-wise Analysis: Get insights into accident data for each district in Karnataka.

Accident Severity Prediction Model

Our state-of-the-art prediction model helps foresee the severity of accidents based on multiple factors:

  • Latitude and Longitude
  • District Name
  • Weather Conditions
  • Accident Location

Output: Predicts the severity of an accident, aiding in quicker and more effective emergency response.


Interactive Chatbot

Our website features an interactive chatbot designed to assist you with queries about accident data. The chatbot can provide instant answers to questions such as "give me the number of accidents in Mangalore," making it easier for you to access specific information quickly and efficiently.


Accident Reporting

We empower the general public to contribute to road safety through our accident reporting feature:

  • Mobile App: If you witness an accident, use our app to report it in real-time. Download our App or Website
  • Data Reflection: All reported accidents are instantly reflected on our website, providing fresh data for continuous analysis and insights.

How It Works

  1. Data Collection: Gather accident data from multiple sources, including public reports and official records.
  2. Analysis and Visualization: Use advanced analytics and visualization tools to present the data in an understandable format.
  3. Prediction: Implement machine learning models to predict accident severity based on real-time data inputs.
  4. Reporting: Enable real-time accident reporting through a user-friendly mobile app.
