Accidental Analysis

Description

Powerful tool designed to assess the likelihood of accidents occurring in a specific area based on user-provided inputs. The accidental analysis model focuses on understanding the root causes of accidents. By analyzing accident data, safety professionals can identify patterns, contributing factors, and risk areas. This knowledge helps authorities take preventive measures and improve safety. In the context of traffic accidents, analytic graphs consider the connectivity of accident events on the road network. Graph metrics like degree centrality and PageRank centrality help rank and profile accident-prone areas over time and space, aiding urban traffic accident analysts in enhancing safety.

We also have made a AI Chat Bot, tailored to provide insights on accidents in Karnataka, offers users a comprehensive understanding of road safety within the region. By utilizing data on accident locations, severity, and contributing factors, the chatbot enables users to inquire about recent incidents, historical trends, and receive safety recommendations. Through conversational interactions and integrated visualizations, the chatbot enhances user engagement and promotes informed decision-making, ultimately contributing to the reduction of accidents and fatalities on Karnataka'sÂ roads.
